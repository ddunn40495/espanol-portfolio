Things About Me

Hi! My name is Autumn Smart and this is my Digital Portfolio. I am currently a senior at Arabia Mountain High School.

After high school I’m hoping to go to college to major in either Psychology or Sociology. Over the past couple years and due to COVID mental health has become an important aspect in my life. My main goal in life after high school is to use my knowledge and whichever degree I choose to help others.

Some of my hobbies are shopping, traveling, and trying new foods, and crocheting.

My favorite genres of music are R & B and HipHop. Some of my favorite artist right now are SZA, Summer Walker, and Bruno Mars

¿Quién soy yo?

In this assignment everyone got the chance to learn more about each other. We recorded videos talking about ourselves. Answered questions such as “ where are you from?” and “ What are your hobbies?” This was a great introduction to the class and a great way for everyone to get to know each other and our interests. It was an opportunity to step outside of your comfort zone if you’re a shy person.

Crear 10 diapositivas de comparativos

In the assignment we had to make a powerpoint presentation on comparisons. Such as, short and tall or older and younger. This assignment was great because it increased my understanding of making comparisons in spanish. When doing this assignment it was tough coming up with different comparisons but once I did, I enjoyed making the presentation for it.

Comparison Presentation

Mi Rutina Diaria

In this assignment we had to create a video of twelve different scenes of either your morning routine or a famous person’s morning routine. I decided to choose a famous person and chose Oprah Winfrey. Throughout the duration of this project I learned a lot about Oprah Winfrey. I think this was my favorite project to work on simply because it was inspiring to see what a successful black woman does during her day.

Oprah Presentation

Pretérito vs. Imperfecto

Practicing Pretérito and Imperfecto was a great way of preparing before our quizzes and our study spanish assignments. Understanding the different between the two helped when forming sentences in spanish and understanding verb usage and placement.

LAS CARRERAS DEL FUTURO

In this assignment we explored jobs that will be in demand in the future. After exploring we listed future careers that interest us in a graphic organizer. I enjoyed this assignment because it encouraged me to do research about jobs/careers that I never looked into before. I also enjoyed doing research about the careers I have interest in and learning more about those as well. In the future I hope to pursue a career with a psychology degree.

Un Evento Actual

In this assignment we looked up current events from Spanish speaking countries. This assignment was interesting because I used DailyMexico.net. This was my first time using a Spanish news site. My current event was about what age group of people mostly move to Mexico. While we may think it's mostly older Americans and retirees it is actually younger families.

Ser locutor(a) de radio

In this assignment we are acting as announcer for a spanish- language radio station, We had to create a name and present a current story. The name of my radio station was “The Talk Radio Station”. My current story was about teachers in Zacatecas, Mexico, protesting about their unpaid salaries. On February 8, the teachers were on strike because they had not been paid for a whole month of labor.

Crear un cartel para un evento

In this assignment we had to create an ad promoting a social action project in our community. I chose to do my social action project on mental health. On my poster I encourage people to lend a helping hand, support your peers, spread kind words, collaborate when working as a team, make colorful signs with positive affirmations, don’t use negativities, and don’t be afraid to ask for help. I enjoyed this assignment because I was able to make a creative poster about a social action project that I am supportive of.

Mental Health Poster

Crear un cartel de protección del medio ambiente

And this assignment we got into groups, my group was of three, and we had to create an environmental protection poster. When beginning the poster we came together to choose a topic that interested us. Our topic was protecting the planet. We then had to come up with a slogan which was “We are the World”. After that we started to create a poster. We decorated our poster and made it look like a solar system. I enjoyed being put into groups and collaborating with other people and putting all our ideas together to create something great.
