Things About Me

¿Quién soy yo?

In this assignment everyone got the chance to learn more about each other. We recorded videos talking about ourselves. Answered questions such as “ where are you from?” and “ What are your hobbies?” This was a great introduction to the class and a great way for everyone to get to know each other and our interests. It was an opportunity to step outside of your comfort zone if you’re a shy person.

Crear 10 diapositivas de comparativos

In the assignment we had to make a powerpoint presentation on comparisons. Such as, short and tall or older and younger. This assignment was great because it increased my understanding of making comparisons in spanish. When doing this assignment it was tough coming up with different comparisons but once I did, I enjoyed making the presentation for it.

Comparison Presentation

Mi Rutina Diaria

In this assignment we had to create a video of twelve different scenes of either your morning routine or a famous person’s morning routine. I decided to choose a famous person and chose Oprah Winfrey. Throughout the duration of this project I learned a lot about Oprah Winfrey. I think this was my favorite project to work on simply because it was inspiring to see what a successful black woman does during her day.

Oprah Presentation

Pretérito vs. Imperfecto

Practicing Pretérito and Imperfecto was a great way of preparing before our quizzes and our study spanish assignments. Understanding the different between the two helped when forming sentences in spanish and understanding verb usage and placement.
